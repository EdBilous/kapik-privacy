Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Introduction

KAPIK ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: KAPIK stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

All your data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Expense records
  • Receipt images and OCR data
  • Categories and budgets
  • Recurring payments
  • Tags and preferences

Camera Access

We request camera access solely for scanning receipts. Images are processed locally on your device using on-device OCR technology and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Photo Library Access

We request photo library access to allow you to select existing images of receipts for scanning. Selected images are processed locally.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use local notifications to remind you about scheduled payments. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. Your data remains on your device and is not synced to any cloud service.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Your financial information stays on your device.

Data Export

You can export your expense data to CSV format. This export is performed locally, and you control how and where to share the exported file.

Third-Party Services

KAPIK uses the following on-device technologies:

  • Google ML Kit — For on-device OCR text recognition (no data sent to Google servers)

Children's Privacy

KAPIK is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data at any time
