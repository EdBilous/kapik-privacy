KAPIK Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Introduction

KAPIK ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Points: KAPIK stores all your data locally on your device by default. If you enable optional AI-powered receipt parsing, OCR text may be sent to third-party AI services (Google Gemini or Groq) for processing.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

All your data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Expense records
  • Receipt images and OCR data
  • Categories and budgets
  • Recurring payments
  • Tags and preferences

Camera Access

We request camera access solely for scanning receipts. Images are processed locally on your device using on-device OCR technology and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Photo Library Access

We request photo library access to allow you to select existing images of receipts for scanning. Selected images are processed locally.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use local notifications to remind you about scheduled payments. No notification data is sent to external servers.

AI Receipt Parsing (Optional)

KAPIK offers an optional AI-powered receipt parsing feature that can improve accuracy when scanning receipts. This feature is disabled by default and requires you to provide your own API key.

When enabled, OCR-extracted text from your receipts may be sent to one of the following AI services for parsing:

  • Google Gemini — Google's AI service
  • Groq — Groq's LLM inference service

Important:

  • Only the text extracted from receipts is sent — not the images themselves
  • You choose which AI provider to use and provide your own API key
  • You can disable AI parsing at any time in Settings
  • Results are cached locally to minimize API requests
  • You can use the app in fully offline mode without any AI features

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. Your data remains on your device and is not synced to any cloud service.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Your financial information stays on your device.

If you enable AI receipt parsing, OCR text is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you selected (Google or Groq) using your own API key. We do not have access to this data or your API keys.

Data Export

You can export your expense data to CSV format. This export is performed locally, and you control how and where to share the exported file.

Third-Party Services

On-Device (No Data Sent)

  • Google ML Kit — For on-device OCR text recognition. All processing happens locally on your device.

Cloud Services (Optional, User-Enabled)

If you enable AI receipt parsing, the following services may be used:

  • Google Gemini API — For AI-powered receipt text parsing. Subject to Google's API Terms.
  • Groq API — Alternative AI provider for receipt parsing. Subject to Groq's Terms of Use.

These services only receive OCR text when you explicitly enable AI parsing and provide your own API key. No data is sent to these services by default.

Children's Privacy

KAPIK is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data at any time
  • Disable AI features and use the app fully offline
  • Choose your preferred AI provider or use none at all
